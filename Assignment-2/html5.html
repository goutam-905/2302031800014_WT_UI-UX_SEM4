Mobile Apps for Plant Identification

  • PlantNet (Free) - Uses image recognition to identify plants from photos.
  • PictureThis (Freemium) - Provides AI-powered identification with additional plant care tips.
  • Naturalist (Free) - Developed by the California Academy of Sciences, helps with plant and wildlife identification.
  • Seek by iNaturalist (Free) - Kid-friendly version of Naturalist, great for exploring plants and animals.
  • Flora Incognita (Free) - AI-based plant recognition, developed for scientific purposes.
